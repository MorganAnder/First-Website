While nicknamed killer whales, Orca's are actually in the dolphin family!
Orca's are matriarcal! There social structures revolve around familys (called pods) that start with the eldest female member. The K Pod, a local PNW group, is headed by Sequim who is 48.
Female orcas are suspected to live up to 80 years... Sequim has a while to go!
