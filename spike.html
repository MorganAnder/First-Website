Fun Orca Facts!

Back to the Main Page

  • While nicknamed "killer whales," orcas are actually members of the dolphin family.
  • Orcas are matriarchal! Their social structures revolve around families (called pods) led by the eldest female.
  • The K Pod, a local PNW group, is headed by Sequim, who is 48 years old.
  • Female orcas are suspected to live up to 80 years... Sequim has a while to go!
